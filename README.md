# Grandma-s-Savory-Chicken-and-Potato-Cobbler-Recipe
Savory chicken recipe 
Chicken: 1 lb boneless breasts or thighs
dicedPotatoes: 1.5 lbs Yukon Gold, peeled and boiled until soft
Tomatoes: 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes or 3 large fresh tomatoes,
choppedCheese: 1.5 cups shredded cheddar or mozzarella
Flour: 1 cup all-purpose flour
Butter: 6 tbsp (4 tbsp for potatoes, 2 tbsp for sautéing)
Milk: 1/2 cup whole milk
Eggs: 1 large egg
Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
Sugar: 1 tbsp
Instructions:
Prepare the Base:In a large skillet, melt 2 tbsp of butter. Sauté the chicken until browned. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.Simmer the Sauce:Stir in the tomatoes and sugar. Simmer on low for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Pour this mixture into a 9x13 baking dish.Make the Potato Topping:In a large bowl, mash the boiled potatoes with 4 tbsp of butter and the milk until smooth.Bind the Crust:Whisk the egg and stir it into the mashed potatoes. Gently fold in the flour and 1 cup of the cheese until you have a thick, doughy potato mixture.Assemble:Drop spoonfuls of the potato dough over the chicken and tomato base in the baking dish. Do not smooth it out; the "cobbles" create a better texture.Bake:Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of cheese over the top. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes, or until the potato topping is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
